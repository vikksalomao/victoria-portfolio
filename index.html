I'm Victória

I am a UX Strategist


I have been working as a strategist since 2016. I started my career as a brand strategist, and I've always been passionate about research and understanding the customers' nuances. In 2019 I realized that what is my passion is to help customers with their experiences with services and products, independent if it is a marketing, branding, UX, or UI problem. For customers, there is only one relationship with a specific brand. They don't understand that inside a company, there are different businesses, and it is their right to want to be recognized as one user because they are not more than one person. Then, my mission is to encourage brands to ensure that the user will have one unique experience. That is why I came to Barcelona to do a Master's in UX Design to connect my business and branding skills with the design process, which has the purpose of solving problems.


The thing that I most enjoy doing is to create jokes and pickup lines with random words. People say they are not funny, but just those who have a peculiar humor laugh at them. Besides that, I love to cook. I try to show my creative side in the kitchen, creating new recipes. Last but not least, I would say that I am the person who plans the parties. I am very sociable, and I communicate with everybody quickly, plus I don't care about spending my time making fun happen if everybody is together.
